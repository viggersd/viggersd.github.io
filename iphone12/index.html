We reduced our carbon footprint.
By thinking inside the box.

Across the iPhone family, we’re removing the power adapter
and EarPods that often go unused, but including the fast‑charging
USB‑C to Lightning cable that most people need. That shrinks packaging,
and more boxes per shipment means fewer shipments overall. We’re also transitioning
our manufacturing partners to renewable energy.
Altogether, this eliminates over 2 million tonnes of carbon emissions annually.
